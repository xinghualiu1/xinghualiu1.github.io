--- layout: single title: "Research Projects" permalink: /research/ author_profile: true --- Selected funded research projects in shared mobility, transport governance, and sustainable urban systems. ## Financing Clean Air: The Potential of Land Value Capture to Secure Sustainable Urban Development and Enhance Air Quality - **Funders:** National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC); Joint Programming Initiative Urban Europe (JPI Urban Europe) - **Partners:** University of Liverpool; Universite Paris-Est; Radboud University; Tongji University; Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University - **Contribution:** Served as student lead for the China team. Coordinated international collaboration, delivered progress updates, and led the China-side empirical analysis linking urban development, environmental quality, and transport-related policy issues. ## Theoretical Research on the Modernization of Urban Traffic Governance - **Funder:** National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) - **Contribution:** Investigated transport resilience and behavioural responses during public-health emergencies using social-media and mobility-related data. Contributed to policy-oriented analyses on public attitudes, equity, and governance. ## Carbon Emissions Trading for Road Traffic: Mechanism Design under Government-Enterprise-Resident Co-governance - **Funder:** National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) - **Contribution:** Analysed shared-mobility travel behaviour and developed quantitative methods to estimate the environmental impacts of ride-splitting and other shared transport services. ## Big-Data-Driven Paradigm Shift of Management and Decision on Shared Mobility - **Funder:** National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) - **Contribution:** Developed trip-identification methods for ride-splitting based on large-scale mobility records and designed a data-driven framework to evaluate environmental and behavioural impacts of shared mobility services.

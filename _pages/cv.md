Professional Profile
======
Postdoctoral researcher specializing in shared mobility analysis, prediction, and optimization using spatiotemporal data analytics and explainable AI. My research integrates statistical modelling, causal inference, spatial analysis, and interpretable machine learning to generate transparent, reliable, and robust insights into travel behaviour and urban mobility systems.

Professional Experience
======
* Postdoctoral Researcher, Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering, Universite Laval, Quebec City, Canada, January 2026 to present
  * Developing data-driven methods for shared mobility analysis using GPS-based spatiotemporal datasets and explainable AI
  * Focusing on transparency, reliability, and robustness in urban mobility analytics

Research Interests
======
* Topics: Shared mobility, ride-hailing, ride-sharing, bike-sharing, micromobility, public transit integration, multimodal travel behaviour, urban mobility patterns, sustainable transport planning, transportation equity and inclusion
* Methods: GPS trajectory analysis, statistical modelling, causal inference, spatial analysis, spatial econometrics, discrete choice modelling, interpretable machine learning, explainable AI, GeoAI, deep learning, optimization
* Data: GPS-based mobility traces, transit smart-card transactions, social media data, land-use and POI data, housing price data, air-quality data, mobile-phone signaling data, and other multi-source spatiotemporal urban datasets
